Navigation bar

A navigation bar is a user interface element commonly found on websites and web applications. It provides users with a way to navigate between different sections, pages, or functionalities of a website. The navigation bar is typically located at the top of the page (header), but it can also appear on the side or bottom depending on the design and layout.
